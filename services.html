Services

Below are a few examples of services I can provide.

Impact assessment

Impact assessment brings together organizations and communities by acknowledging past successes and current challenges. I can create reports and other products that characterize an organization's impact on public knowledge and well-being.

Example: A research institute wants to start a new research division, but first they wish to understand what's worked in the past and who might be affected by the envisioned changes.

Diligence

As research landscapes and funder requirements evolve, staying aligned can become a complex task. I help organizations bridge the gap between high-level policy and daily workflows.

Example: Funders mandate that research code must be open source, and a research organization wishes to ensure that their new AI research follows open source best practices.

Discovery

Often, different teams in a research organization are sharing their discoveries in disparate places. I can uncover where your research teams are sharing their work and where there are opportunities for convergence.

Example: Researchers may be sharing data through personal accounts on unsanctioned repositories because the organization's current infrastructure does not support files of a certain size, or does not permit easy browsing.

Facilitation

As organizations grow, valuable insights can become siloed. I can facilitate the formation of communities of practice and/or governance structures to formalize knowledge-sharing and collaboration.

Example: An organization would like to serve as the nucleus for an international professional community in a certain field, but lacks formal structures for delegating decision-making authority.
