Services

Below are a few examples of services I can provide.

Impact assessment

I evaluate past successes and current challenges to help you navigate institutional change with confidence.

Example: Assessing organizational history and stakeholder impact before launching a new research division.

Policy and Diligence

I bridge the gap between high-level mandates and daily workflows, ensuring your team stays ahead of evolving requirements.

Example: Aligning AI research projects with new funder mandates for open source code and data.

Discovery

I identify where your teams share their work and where there are opportunities for either cataloging or convergence.

Example: Transitioning researchers from sharing data through personal accounts to robust institutional infrastructure.

Facilitation

I transform siloed expertise into formal communities of practice with governance structures that scale.

Example: Establishing decision-making procedures for an organization that would like to serve as the nucleus for an international professional community.
