Services

Below are just a few examples of the types of services I can provide.

Impact assessment

Funders and research orgnizations want to know the impact of their activities on public knowledge and well-being. Communities want funders and research organizations to understand their needs. Impact assessment improves communication between organizations and communities by highlighting successes as well as barriers toward achieving mutual goals. Example: An independent research institute wants to start a new research division, so they need impact analysis to understand what's worked in the past and who might be affected by the envisioned changes.

Diligence

As technologies and funder policies evolve, research organizations must ensure their scientists are fulfilling their open science obligations. Organizations may also have internal policies that are unclear, misunderstood, or outdated. I can help you understand whether policies are being followed, what the barriers to compliance might be, and a path to concordance between policy and practice. Example: Funding agencies mandate that code must be open source, but it is unclear where code is being shared or what licenses are being used.
