Today, we call it open science.

Tomorrow, we'll just call it science.

Now more than ever, we need open science. We need a society of investigators, explorers, and tinkerers, driven by integrity and a love of learning.

Originally trained in plant genetics and genomics, I now do research that helps organizations plan their open science strategy. I can discover, collect, and communicate trends in your organization, so you can make informed decisions. Through impact assessment, auditing, surveys, focus groups, or outreach, I can to help you make sense of the open science landscape, and help your researchers connect with a broader audience.
