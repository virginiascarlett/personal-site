Virginia Scarlett

Open science researcher

Originally trained in plant genetics and genomics, I now do research that empowers organizations to advance their open science strategy. Through surveys, interviews, data audits, and more, I can help you design programs that align with your values and connect your researchers with a broader audience.

My work doesn’t stop at a final report. I'll partner with stakeholders in your organization to turn research insights into action. Through my experiences with community management, teaching, and facilitation, I've learned how to assemble communities of practice around the issues I investigate, cutting across silos and hierarchies.

To me, organizational research is not about predicting the future or eliminating risk. It's about elevating the good ideas that are often lost in the busy-ness of business-as-usual.
