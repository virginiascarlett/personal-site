Today, we call it open science.

Tomorrow, we'll just call it science.

Now more than ever, we need open science. We need a society of investigators, explorers, and tinkerers, driven by integrity and a love of learning.

Originally trained in plant genetics and genomics, I now do research that helps organizations plan their open science strategy. Through impact assessment, auditing, landscape analyses or surveys, I can help you design programs that align with your values and help your researchers connect with a broader audience.

I am also a community manager, creating educational resources and assembling communities of practice for the issues I investigate.
