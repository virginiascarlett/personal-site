Today, we call it open science.

Tomorrow, we'll just call it science.

Now more than ever, we need open science. We need a society of investigators, explorers, and tinkerers, driven by integrity and a love of learning. And we need infrastructure to support their efforts.

I do research that helps organizations plan their open science strategy. By collecting data and suggesting low-risk experiments, I can help your organization offer more engaging science experiences for users, while promoting best practices among your researchers.
